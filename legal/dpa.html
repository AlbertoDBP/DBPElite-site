Data Processing Agreement

How athlete personal data is processed, stored, and protected

Effective Date: May 6, 2026  ·  Digital Business Partners LLC  ·  DBP Elite Athlete Soccer Profile  ·  Miami, Florida

Note: This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") supplements the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. It is provided for transparency and GDPR readiness. International subscribers in the EEA may request a countersigned DPA by contacting us.

1. Scope

This DPA applies to all personal data processed by Digital Business Partners LLC ("DBP," "Processor") on behalf of the Subscriber ("Controller") in connection with the DBP Elite Athlete Soccer Profile service. It covers data submitted via the athlete intake process, including minor athlete data provided by parents or guardians.

2. Data Processed

  • Athlete name, age, position, club, graduation year, GPA
  • Athletic performance statistics and awards
  • Photographs and video highlights
  • Personal bio and character narrative
  • Subscriber contact information (name, email)
  • Billing information (processed directly by ZOHO Billing; DBP does not store payment card data)

3. Purpose of Processing

DBP processes personal data exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Designing, building, and hosting the athlete's public recruitment profile
  • Managing the Subscriber's account and subscription
  • Providing customer support and profile updates
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Sub-Processors

DBP uses the following sub-processors, each bound by appropriate data protection agreements:

  • ZOHO Billing (Zoho Corporation) — subscription management and payment processing. ZOHO is GDPR-compliant and SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • Hostinger International Ltd. — web hosting and file storage. Data hosted in the United States.
  • Google Fonts (Google LLC) — font delivery. No personal data is transmitted.

DBP will notify Subscribers of any material changes to sub-processors with at least 14 days' notice.

5. Data Security Measures

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Access controls limiting athlete data to authorized DBP personnel only
  • No payment card data stored by DBP (all payment data handled by ZOHO Billing)
  • Regular review of hosting and sub-processor security posture

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Athlete profile data is retained for the active subscription term plus 12 months. Subscribers may request deletion at any time. Upon confirmed deletion request, DBP will remove all athlete personal data from active systems within 30 days and from backups within 90 days, except where retention is required by law.

7. Minor Data Special Handling

All athlete data involves minors. DBP applies heightened care to this data category: access is limited to authorized staff involved in profile production, data is not used for advertising or sold to third parties, and public display occurs only with verified parental authorization.

8. Data Transfers

Data is hosted in the United States. For Subscribers in the EEA or UK, international transfers are made under appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable). Contact us to request applicable transfer documentation.

9. Subscriber Rights

Subscribers acting as data controllers may exercise access, correction, deletion, portability, and objection rights by contacting info@dbpelite.com. We will respond within 10 business days.

10. Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach affecting Subscriber or athlete data, DBP will notify affected Subscribers within 72 hours of becoming aware of the breach, to the extent required by applicable law.
